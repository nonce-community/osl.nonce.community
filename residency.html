nonce residency program

nonce residency program supports open source blockchain developers by providing them with grant of up to $3,000/month with free access to nonce co-living & working space located in Seoul, Korea.

It is highly flexible. You can get funded for contributing to the open source blockchain projects that you love! You will engage with local blockchain community by regularly conducting lectures, publishing articles and living and working together with local community members. If you are looking for exciting new opportunity to engage with Korean crypto community, pre-register for our residency program. We will notify you when the application process begins.
